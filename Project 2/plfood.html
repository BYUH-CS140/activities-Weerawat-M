Pad Thai

Pad Thai is one of Thailand’s most famous stir-fried noodle dishes, made with rice noodles, eggs, tofu or shrimp, bean sprouts, and peanuts. It’s flavored with tamarind sauce, lime, and chili, offering a perfect balance of sweet, sour, and savory tastes.

Krapao (Thai Basil Stir-Fry)

Krapao is a spicy Thai stir-fry dish made with minced meat (often pork or chicken), Thai basil leaves, garlic, and chili. Served over rice and usually topped with a fried egg, it’s a popular everyday street food known for its bold, savory flavor.

Tom Yum Kung

Tom Yum Kung is a hot and sour Thai soup made with shrimp, lemongrass, galangal, kaffir lime leaves, chili, and lime juice. It's aromatic, spicy, and tangy, and is one of Thailand’s most iconic and beloved soups.
